My Career Journey

Getting into ABA

After graduating high school my plans where to enroll in the military. Due to medical issues my military dreams were short lived and I had to find a job because I did not have plans to go to college initially; I missed all deadlines for emrollment. while looking for employment I came across a position called 'Registered Behavior Technician', I applied as an entry level Behavior Technician, I got the job due to my experience working with autistic kids during my internship my senior year. I began training a month later and been working for ABA Centers of America ever since.
